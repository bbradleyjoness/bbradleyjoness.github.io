Help move projects forward.

Strong local projects need people, partners, and practical resources: volunteers, meeting support, tactical materials, outreach, and neighborhood coordination.

If you want to support projects directly, we’re especially looking for:

  • Volunteers for build days, outreach, and tabling
  • Tools and temporary tactical materials for pilots
  • Partners for gardening, mobility, and public space events
  • Local sponsors for supplies, signage, and safety support

Use the contact form below and choose “Join / support a project” so we can connect you with an active project team.
